I had the fortune of meeting this awesome fish at the Santa Marta Aquarium in Colombia. They are a Spot-fin Porcupinefish (Diodon Holocanthus Hystrix). They are often confused with Pufferfish, since both of them can blow up when they feel threatened, with either air or water. However, Porcupinefish, as can be seen in the video, have an extra layer of protection in the prominent, sharp spines that are visible even when the fish is not inflated, unlike Pufferfish which are cover in prickly bumps that are usually only visible when they are inflated.
